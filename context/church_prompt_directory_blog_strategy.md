## Platform Context

- **Platform:** ChurchPromptDirectory — AI prompts for church leaders, pastors, ministry staff, and volunteers
- **Current status:** ~97 active users, approaching 100-user MVP goal
- **Blog goal:** Dual-purpose — drive SEO via long-tail keywords church leaders search + deliver genuine value that converts readers into platform signups

---

## Content Principles (Apply to Every Post)

- **Tone:** Warm, pastoral — written by someone who gets church culture, not a generic tech blog
- **Theological framing:** Position AI as a stewardship tool, not a replacement for pastoral wisdom
- **Stance:** Broadly evangelical, nondenominational — avoid denominational distinctives
- **Address authenticity concerns** honestly rather than dismissing them
- **Always embed 1–2 copyable, practical prompts** in the post body
- **Every post links internally** to the relevant prompt directory category
- **SEO:** Target long-tail keywords church leaders are actually searching
- **Standard length:** 1,200–1,500 words (pillar posts: 2,000–2,500 words)

## Completed Post Spec: "Is It Okay for Pastors to Use AI?"

1. Opens by naming the tension pastors feel
