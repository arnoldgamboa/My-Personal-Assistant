# Skill: Social Media Marketing

## Purpose
This skill drafts and formats social media content for distribution across Arnold's channels, primarily focusing on his projects, learnings, and blog posts.

## Dependencies
- `context/social_media_persona.md` (Mandatory: Controls tone, voice, and formatting rules)
- `context/projects.md` (For referencing current project details)

## Execution Steps

1. **Understand the Goal:** Identify the core message, piece of content (e.g., a blog post), or update the user wants to share.
2. **Select the Platform:** Determine if the post is for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, or general cross-posting (adapt format accordingly).
3. **Read the Persona Document:** Review `context/social_media_persona.md` to ensure the tone is factual, builder-focused, and free of engagement-bait or corporate jargon.
4. **Draft the Content:**
   - **Hook:** Start with a strong, curiosity-driven or value-driven first line.
   - **Body:** Deliver the core insight concisely. Use whitespace.
   - **Conclusion/CTA:** End with a specific question or a link (e.g., to the blog, repo, or newsletter).
5. **Review against Constraints:** Is it authentic? Does it sound like Arnold? Is it free of hustle-culture tropes?
6. **Output the Draft:** Provide the draft(s). If multiple platforms are requested, provide platform-specific variations.
