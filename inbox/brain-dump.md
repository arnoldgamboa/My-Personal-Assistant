# Brain Dump Inbox

> Staging area for unprocessed brain dump items.
> All entries here are temporary — route them during your next weekly review or anytime.
> Managed as part of the Obsidian Vault. See also: [[skills/brain_dump/SKILL]]

---

## How Items End Up Here

When a brain dump contains items that can't be immediately routed (unclear project, missing context, needs follow-up), they're parked here with a date stamp.

---

## Format

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — [Type: Raw Thoughts / Idea / Meeting Notes / Decide Later / Full Dump]
- [item]
- [item]
```
